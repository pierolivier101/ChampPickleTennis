# 🏆 Champion & Ace: Player Handbook

Welcome to the **Champion & Ace** tournament! This handbook contains everything you need to know to access the league app, schedule your matches, report scores, and climb the leaderboard.

---

## 🔑 1. League Access & Login

To maintain control over tournament participation, accounts are managed exclusively by the league Administrator.

1. **Registration**: The Administrator will register you in the system using your name and will provide you with a randomly generated **4-Digit PIN**.
2. **Accessing the App**: Navigate to the tournament link provided by the admin.
3. **Logging In**: 
   - Select the **Player** tab on the login screen.
   - Start typing your name to select it from the list.
   - Enter your **4-Digit PIN** and click **Access Dashboard**.

---

## 👤 2. Customizing Your Profile

Once logged in, click **Edit Profile** on your dashboard to customize your player profile:
* **Contact Information**: Enter your email, phone number, or handle so other players can reach out to schedule matches.
* **Court Availability**: Specify your typical free times (e.g., "Weekends & Evenings" or "Mondays 5:00 PM - 8:00 PM").
* **Skill Level**: Set your level (Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced) to help schedule balanced match-ups.
* **Short Bio**: Add a custom bio to introduce yourself to the league!

---

## 📅 3. Scheduling Matches (Book & Invite)

You can schedule and book matches with any player in the league directly through the app:

1. Navigate to the **Matches** (Calendar) tab.
2. Select an available date on the calendar.
3. Under **Create Match/Invite**:
   - Choose your opponent from the dropdown list.
   - Select the sport (**Tennis** or **Pickleball**).
   - Enter the date (DD-MM) and time (HH:MM).
   - Select or type a court location (e.g., "Court 1", "Main Court").
   - Click **Send Match Invitation**.
4. **Accepting Invites**: Your opponent will see a pending invite on their dashboard. Once they click **Accept**, the match status changes to **Upcoming** and is officially scheduled on the calendar.

---

## 📝 4. Score Reporting & Points System

After playing your match, either player can submit the scores:

1. On your **Dashboard**, find the match under **Upcoming Matches**.
2. Click **Submit Score**.
3. Enter the set scores.
   * 🎾 **Tennis Format**: 
     - **Sets 1 & 2**: First to **4 games** wins the set. No 2-game difference is required, and there are no tiebreaks (e.g., set scores can be 4-3, 4-2, 4-1, 4-0).
     - **Set 3**: If sets are tied 1-1, a deciding **long tiebreak to 10 points** (win by 2, e.g., 10-8, 12-10) is played as the third set. Leave Set 3 blank if a player wins in straight sets (2-0).
   * 🏓 **Pickleball Format**:
     - **Sets 1 & 2**: First to **11 points** wins the set (win by 2, e.g., 11-9, 12-10).
     - **Set 3**: If sets are tied 1-1, a deciding third set is played to **11 points** (win by 2). **Rally Scoring** is used for this set: every rally won earns a point regardless of who is serving. The serving team changes when the serving team loses a rally. Leave Set 3 blank if a player wins in straight sets (2-0).
4. Click **Confirm**. The system automatically validates, computes the winner, updates standings, and updates points.

### 📊 Point Allocations
Rankings are calculated dynamically based on a point accumulation system:
* **Win**: **3 Points**
* **Loss**: **1 Point**
* **Unplayed / Cancelled**: **0 Points**

*Playing matches consistently is the best way to earn points and climb the ranks!*

---

## 💬 5. Locker Room & Live Chat

Connect and communicate with other players in the **Chat** (Banter Board) tab:
* **Locker Room**: A public feed visible to all tournament participants. Use it for friendly banter, posting match updates, or open callouts for games.
* **Direct Messages**: Click on any player in the user list to start a private chat, perfect for sharing contact info and scheduling details.

---

## 💡 Pro Tips for Players
* **Check Availability**: Before sending a match invitation, click on your opponent's name in the **Ranks** tab to see their preferred availability.
* **Export Calendars**: You can export scheduled matches directly to your Google Calendar or download an `.ics` file for Apple/Outlook calendars from your dashboard.
* **Admin Support**: If a score was submitted incorrectly by mistake, contact the Administrator (via the admin panel) to correct or edit match results.
